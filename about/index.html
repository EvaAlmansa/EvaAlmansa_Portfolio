About

I am a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Early Stage Researcher in the Visual Computing (ViC) group at the Center for Advanced Studies, Research, and Development in Sardinia (CRS4). I hold a Bachelor Degree in Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence (2015) and a Master Degree of Data Science (2017) from the University of Granada, Spain. I am awarded a fellowship (2020-2023) from the EVOCATION innovative training network under H2020.

  • Eva Almansa | Semantic Scholar

  • Techniques: Deep Learning applied from a single panoramic image to estimate depth maps, estimate 3D mesh model of the boundary surfaces of the architectural layout, RGB-D empty room estimation, and sparse to dense depth estimation.

My research supervisor within EVOCATION and at CRS4 are Dr. Enrico Gobbetti, director of Visual and Data-intensive Computing at CRS4, and Dr. Giovanni Pintore, senior research engineer in the same group, while my academic supervisor is Prof. Riccardo Scateni, professor of Computer Science at UniCa.
